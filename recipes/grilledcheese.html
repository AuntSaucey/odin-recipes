Grilled Cheese Sandwhich

Description

Want a quick and easy lunch? Try this yummy grilled cheese sandwhich!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter (enough to spread on the bread)
  • Optional additions: tomatoe slices, cooked bacon, ham

Steps

  1. Lay out on the 2 slices of bread.
  2. Place the cheese slices on one pice of bread. If you're adding anything extra like tomato or bacon, place them on top of the cheese.
  3. Top with the second slice of bread.
  4. Spread butter evenly on the outside of each slice of bread. Make sure to cover the entire surface for a crispy, golden crust.
  5. Heat a skillet or frying pan over medium heat.
  6. Once the pan is hot, place the sandwich in the pan, buttered side down.
  7. Cook for about 2-4 minutes until the bottom is golden brown.
  8. Carefully flip the sandwich, pressing down slightly with a spatula, and cook the other side until it's also golden brown and the cheese is melted, about another 2-4 minutes.
  9. Remove the sandwich from the pan and let it sit for a minute before cutting.
  10. Cut the sandwich in half and serve hot.
