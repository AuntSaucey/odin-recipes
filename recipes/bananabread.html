Banana Bread

Description

I really enjoy making banana bread for all my coworkers and friends. This is a easy to follow recipe everyone you know will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup(1 stick) unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. Mix Wet Ingredients: In a medium bowl, mash the bananas with a fork. Add melted butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and sour cream. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Combine Dry Ingredients: In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Combine Wet and Dry: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  5. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Allow to cool completely.
