Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

Description

This is a super delicious, cheap dinner idea for you and your family.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles(14 ounces for 4 servings)
  • 2 tablespoons olibe oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed or diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil or italian seasoning (optional)
  • Grated parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to as boil.
  2. Add spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes).
  3. Drain the spaghetti and set aside, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  4. In a large skillet or saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the minced garlic and saute for about 1 minute until fragant, but be careful not to burn.
  6. Pour in the can ofn crushed or diced tomatoes. Add salt, pepper, and dried basil or italian seasoning if using.
  7. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes. If it's too thick, add a bit of the reserved pasta water.
  8. Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce. Toss well to ensure the spaghetti is evenly coated with the sauce.
  9. Serve hot. Optionally, sprinkle grated parmesan cheese to top.
